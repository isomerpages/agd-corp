#### Exchange of Best Practices on State Treasury Functions with the Indonesia Ministry of Finance

**29 April 2021**

Singapore enjoys a long-standing and multi-faceted relationship with Indonesia. In the spirit of strengthening bilateral cooperation, AGD hosted about 60 delegates from the Directorate General of Treasury of the Indonesia Ministry of Finance (IMOF) on 27 Apr 2021 through a virtual conference to exchange best practices on state treasury functions as part of IMOFâ€™s benchmarking exercise.  
  
AGD presented on our approach and considerations for: (i) Government Payment Platform (electronic invoicing and central cashless payment); (ii) Data Analytics; and (iii) Public Sector Finance Transformation within the Singapore Public Service
